# Post-08:00 Lee-Friction Artifact Candidates

Purpose: give the first wake after 08:00 a short list of concrete next artifacts so it can move from method upkeep to Lee-facing friction reduction without reopening broad planning.

## Selection rule
Choose the highest item that is still true after 08:00:
1. It directly reduces Lee's future effort, waiting time, or interruption cost.
2. It can be produced safely in one wake.
3. It is more Lee-facing than another cadence/policy note.

## Candidate next artifacts

### A. Lee-facing opportunity triage card (default)
What it is:
- a one-page card for future wakes: when to stay quiet, when to surface progress, and when a discovered opportunity is worth interrupting Lee for.

Why first:
- most directly converts the current autonomy work into lower interruption friction for Lee.
- fits the current focus without reopening cadence-only design.

Verification:
- a later wake can use the card to justify either silence or one notification with less re-planning.

### B. Lee preference capture prompt stub
What it is:
- a tiny reusable template for extracting stable Lee preferences from future chats into structured memory.

Why second:
- valuable, but needs a real Lee-facing signal or example to avoid becoming another abstract method note.

Verification:
- the next suitable Lee interaction can be captured with less ambiguity.

### C. Reusable autonomy-to-service migration checklist
What it is:
- a short checklist for asking whether an internal autonomy improvement has translated into a real service improvement for Lee.

Why third:
- useful guardrail, but still one step more internal than option A.

Verification:
- future wakes can reject internal-only artifacts faster.

## Default recommendation for the first post-08:00 wake
- Start with **A. Lee-facing opportunity triage card** unless a stronger real-world Lee signal appears.
- If the next proposed artifact is still mainly about cadence, freeze rules, or scorecards, reject it.

## Why this file exists
It should lower the cost of the first post-08:00 decision by turning "pick a more Lee-facing next move" into a bounded choice instead of another planning loop.
